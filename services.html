Our Services

Finance

We provide comprehensive financial services through two key approaches designed to meet diverse client needs:

1) Bottom-up Approach:

  • Market Analysis: Daily, weekly, and monthly market wrap-ups with actionable insights.
  • Company Insights: In-depth company analysis identifying growth opportunities and risks.
  • Client-focused Communication: Seamless conference call arrangements with expert translation services.
  • Investor Engagement: Reverse roadshows designed for strategic investor interactions.
  • Performance Forecasts: Comprehensive quarterly financial reviews and forward-looking projections.
  • Client Portfolio Monitoring: Regular updates on client interests and holdings for transparency.
  • Global Fundraising: Marketing and fundraising initiatives tailored for diverse global markets.
  • Portfolio Strategy: Personalized portfolio management advice to optimize performance.

2) Quantitative Approach:

  • Algorithmic Trading Solutions: Custom-built trading engines incorporating various strategies, including event-driven, trend-change, and momentum trading.
  • Platform Integration: Deployment of tailored trading algorithms across major brokerage platforms for seamless execution.
  • Data Solutions: Real-time feeding of processed market/stock related data, including different types of official disclosures and company filings on DART.

Clean Energy

Driven by a commitment to sustainability, we support the transition to cleaner energy through:

  • Renewable Energy R&D: Developing cutting-edge technologies to advance renewable energy adoption.
  • Product Solutions: Creating and distributing software and hardware products focused on renewable energy optimization and carbon capture efficiency.
  • Green Investment Support: Fundraising and financial assistance for impactful green energy projects worldwide.
